Meeting - Parsing - Regex and Grammars

Tags: meeting

Posted on 2014-07-04

This month, Doug Bell from Bank of America will be giving a talk on Parsing using plain regular expressions, the venerable Parse::RecDescent, and the modern Marpa::R2.

The examples given will be based on App::YAML::Filter, a mini-language like the jq document query language that allows filtering and creating YAML documents.
