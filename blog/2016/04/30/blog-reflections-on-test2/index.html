Blog: Reflections on Test2

Tags: blog testing

Posted on 2016-04-30

Joel Berger wrote a blog post reflecting on Test2 Chad Granum's (Exodist) new core test library. (Read more about Test2 on Metacpan).

In his post, Joel explains the rationale behind Test2, the history that has led to this situation, and the path forward getting Test2 into the Perl core.
